Our Story

Since 2018

Founded in 2018 in Singapore, Nexarion emerged from a simple observation: small and medium businesses struggled to find reliable international trade partners.

Our founders, experienced exporters themselves, built a platform that prioritizes trust, transparency, and efficiency in every transaction.

Today, we connect over 12,000 verified suppliers with buyers across 85 countries, facilitating over $50 million in trade monthly.

From our headquarters in Singapore and regional offices in Dubai, Hong Kong, and São Paulo, we continue to expand globally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What are your response times?

Email inquiries: 2-4 hours during business hours. Live chat: instant responses 24/7. Phone calls: immediate during business hours.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes! Our technical team handles platform issues, integrations, and troubleshooting. Use live chat for immediate help or email for detailed queries.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation?

Absolutely! Click "Book a Meeting" above or select "Sales & Pricing" in the contact form to schedule a personalized demo with our team.

How can I become a partner?

Email partnerships@nexarion.com or fill out the form selecting "Partnership Opportunities." We'll schedule a call to discuss collaboration.

Do you provide support in multiple languages?

Yes! We offer support in English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, and 7+ other languages. Specify your preference when contacting us.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

We recommend scheduling ahead, but walk-ins are welcome during business hours. Call us first to ensure the right team member is available.
